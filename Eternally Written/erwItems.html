Item List

This is split into a multitude of categories, including equipable items and consumables. Each character may equip 1 weapon, 1 chest, 1 head, 1 arm, 1 legs, and 1 accessory. Each character has at least 10 inventory slots. Characters can constantly swap items equipped with non-equipped items.
